UX Audit

We don’t just find issues—we show you how to fix them and boost performance through smarter usability

Trusted by companies like

When UX Audit is essential

Low transaction rates

A drop in performance (not seasonally driven) may point to issues in navigation, layout, or overall experience

Decrease in organic visibility

UX signals, including user behavior and commercial intent, influence search rankings

Upcoming website redesign

An audit helps identify usability issues early, so they’re not carried into the new design

Pre-launch of advertising campaigns

A usability review ensures your site is optimized to convert increased traffic and prevents wasted ad spend

Bad Audit

A typical 50-page list of standard issues from a checklist

Good Audit

Recommendations are based on data and expert analysis, and come with a clear, actionable plan for implementation

What Our Ux Audit Covers

01. Competitive analysis
Is your site clearly communicating value to the right audience? We assess brand positioning, messaging clarity, and layout logic in a competitive context. We use the 80/20 principle to make sure we're focusing on the 20% of actions that will yield 80% of your results, so we can have the greatest impact in the least amount of time.
02. Content Quality
We evaluate whether your content is useful, relevant, and easy to understand—supporting both user needs and business goals.
03. User’s Journey
We analyze how key user segments navigate your site, identifying blockers, confusion points, and unnecessary steps along the conversion path.
04. Conversion Flow
From search to checkout, we review how smooth and intuitive the purchase process is—including filters, search, cart, and forms.
05. Cross-Device Performance
We check how your site performs across screens and devices—ensuring a consistent, user-friendly experience everywhere.

Our Workflow

1

Briefing & Website Review

We begin by reviewing your site and verifying that analytics are properly configured. If needed, we’ll provide a technical brief for adjustments—accurate data is the foundation of a meaningful audit

2

Manual Analysis Using Analytics Tools

We manually assess key entry points, category pages, and product pages using analytics data. Based on this, we provide structural recommendations focused on key visual blocks, trust elements, and mobile-specific engagement triggers

3

Post-Audit Support

We provide support for one month after delivery to review implementation and assist with any follow-up on our recommendations
