Frequent questions about split-testing

  • What are the service rates?

    We calculate the cost of our services based on the budget you plan to spend on advertising and the guarantees we include in the contract.

  • Who is responsible for the creatives for the campaign?

    We work with the client's creatives. We also develop our own. We make as many creatives as necessary to test all the experts' hypotheses. We do both static and dynamic, including video. We calculate the cost of our services based on the budget you plan to spend on advertising and the guarantees we include in the contract.

  • I already have campaigns running. Is there room for improvement?

    Yes, we can. We are often approached with existing campaigns and the work of rebuilding or creating new campaigns is a fundamental task for the team. We start by reviewing existing campaigns, highlighting strengths and weaknesses and making recommendations to optimise targeting, bids and ads. Our favourite job is turning good into great.

  • Do you have any case studies in my area of interest?

    Our agency has been working in performance marketing for 17 years. During this time we have gained experience in all the key niches. Our clients include banks, car dealers, dentists, transport companies, furniture stores, game and app developers. On the website you can see some of the cases we have worked on. The manager will select and show you projects that are as close as possible to your business.
